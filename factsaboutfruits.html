10 FUN FACTS ABOUT FRUITS

1. In the U.S., the apples sold at stores can be up to a year old.

2. A strawberry isn't an actual berry, but a banana is.

3. Grapes explode when you put them in the microwave.

4. Apples, peaches and raspberries are all members of the rose family.

5. Oranges are not even in the top ten list of common foods when it comes to vitamin C levels.

6. Bananas are slightly radioactive.

7. A pineapple is not an "apple" it is actually a berry.

8. Oranges are the largest citrus crop in the world.

9. An apple tree will start bearing fruit 8-10 years after it is planted.

10. If you bite into an apple you would expect to find the "seeds" inside. With the strawberry, the "seeds" are on the outside. Actually, strawberry seeds aren't really seeds. They are 'achenes', which are actually tiny fruits that contain seeds.
